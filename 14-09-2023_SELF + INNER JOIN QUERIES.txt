Q1. WAQTD ENAME, EDNAME, MNAME,MDANME OF EMP IF EMP AND MANAGER WORKING IN SAME LOCATION 'NEW YORK'.

SQL> SELECT E.ENAME,M.ENAME MNAME, D.DNAME ENAME, F.DNAME MDNAME
  2   FROM EMP E, EMP M, DEPT D, DEPT F
  3   WHERE E.MGR = M.EMPNO
  4   AND E.DEPTNO =D.DEPTNO
  5   AND M.DEPTNO = F.DEPTNO
  6  AND D.LOC = 'NEW YORK'

Q2. WAQTD ENAME, MNAME, ELOC, MLOC IF EMP AND MANAGER WORKS IN SAME DEPTNAME ACCOUNTING AND EMP GETTING SAL LESS THAN MANAGER.

SQL> SELECT E.ENAME,M.ENAME MNAME,D.LOC ELOC, F.LOC MLOC
  2  FROM EMP E, EMP M, DEPT D, DEPT F
  3  WHERE E.MGR = M.EMPNO
